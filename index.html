Joint Youth Ministry is organizing an overnight which is due to take place at Daylight Church Kamuli B and with great delight and humility request you to minister in this overnight which is due to take place on the 28th of April 2023 from 7:30 pm to 5:00 am. We have invited the youths of all churches in Kamuli and Naalya and beyond, youth choirs, and gospel artists for this gracious overnight. We believe God will touch the youth on that night.
